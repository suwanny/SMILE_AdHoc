A plant that was kept inside a house all winter was placed outside in full sunlight in the spring. The leaves of this plant soon died, and new leaves grew in their place. The new leaves were greener and thicker. Which hypothesis is best supported by this information?

(1) Plants can learn to stay healthy.
(2) Plants can adapt to some changes.
(3) Plants can change leaves at any time.
(4) Plants can produce weather changes.

Your answer : (2)
Right answer : (2)
